Your Name (Kimi no Na wa.)

Directed by Makoto Shinkai, Your Name is a breathtaking romantic fantasy film. It follows two teenagers, Taki from Tokyo and Mitsuha from a rural town, who mysteriously swap bodies and form a deep connection without meeting each other. As they search for answers, a stunning twist involving a comet and time itself changes everything.

With gorgeous animation, unforgettable music by RADWIMPS, and emotional storytelling, it's one of the most beloved anime films ever made.

Jujutsu Kaisen



Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who becomes the host of a powerful Curse after swallowing a cursed object. To protect others and control the evil within him, he joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers. The anime is known for its intense battles, stylish animation, and a deep supernatural world filled with curses and dark energy.

Demon Slayer



Demon Slayer tells the emotional journey of Tanjiro Kamado, who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. Together, they travel across Japan to hunt demons and find a cure. With breathtaking visuals, sword-fighting action, and heartfelt storytelling, this anime has become a global phenomenon.

Death Note



Death Note follows Light Yagami, a brilliant student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. As he takes on the role of a god-like figure to rid the world of criminals, he is pursued by the genius detective L. The series explores morality, justice, and the psychological consequences of unlimited power.
